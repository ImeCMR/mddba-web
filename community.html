MDDB America is built by the community, for the community. Join us in advancing open molecular dynamics data science.

Ways to Contribute

Whether you are a researcher, developer, or institution, there are many ways to contribute and benefit from the MDDB America initiative.

🗄️

MDDB America

Open Molecular Dynamics Data Platform

🔬

Share Your Data

Contribute your molecular dynamics simulation datasets to the repository and make your research more impactful and reproducible.

💻

Develop Tools

Help build and improve the tools, APIs, and infrastructure that power the MDDB America platform.

📣

Spread the Word

Help grow our community by sharing MDDB America with colleagues, at conferences, and in your publications.

✉️

Join the Mailing List

Stay informed about new datasets, platform updates, events, and opportunities to get involved.

Cite Our Work

If you use MDDB America data in your research, please cite us to help demonstrate the value of open data in science.
